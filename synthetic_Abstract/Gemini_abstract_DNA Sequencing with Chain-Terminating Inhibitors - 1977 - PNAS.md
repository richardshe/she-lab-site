We describe a simple and rapid method for determining nucleotide sequences in DNA using DNA polymerase I. The technique employs 2',3'-dideoxynucleoside triphosphates as specific chain-terminating inhibitors. During in vitro DNA synthesis, these analogs are incorporated but prevent further extension, generating a series of fragments ending at specific bases. Fractionation of these fragments by high-resolution gel electrophoresis produces a sequence pattern that can be directly read.
