A new and rapid method for determining nucleotide sequences in DNA is described. It depends on the use of DNA polymerase to extend a primer along a single-stranded template in the presence of 2',3'-dideoxy or arabinose analogues of the normal deoxyribonucleoside triphosphates, which act as specific chain-terminating inhibitors. The method has been applied to restriction enzyme fragments of bacteriophage phiX174 DNA, and sequences of up to 300 nucleotides have been determined.
