The mechanisms by which body weight is regulated in mammals are poorly understood. We report the positional cloning of the mouse obese (ob) gene and its human homologue. The ob gene encodes a 4.5-kb adipose tissue mRNA with a 167-amino-acid open reading frame whose predicted product has features of a secreted protein. The C57BL/6J ob/ob mutation results from a nonsense substitution at codon 105, while SM/Ckc-Dac ob2J/ob2J mice fail to synthesize ob RNA. ob may encode a signal from adipose tissue regulating body fat mass.
