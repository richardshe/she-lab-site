Join Us

We are recruiting at all levels — undergraduate interns, Ph.D. students, and postdoctoral fellows. I’m especially interested in hearing from postdoc applicants with ambitious, creative, or slightly crazy ideas, even if they fall a little outside of the lab's main focus. If the vision is strong, I’ll find a way to help you make it happen. More broadly, if you're excited about our work on human evolution or organoid-based CRISPR screening, send a CV and a short statement of interests to richardsheshe@gmail.com.
